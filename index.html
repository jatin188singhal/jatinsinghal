HungerDeal | Best Deal for your Meal

HungerDeal was a course project under Dr. Rajiv Ratan Shah to create an Android Application, which could provide an entirely new area for food delivery applications. It was done in a team of four people, where I acted as the UX researcher, designer and front end engineer. This was a development intensive project, but we conducted user research and collected user feedback at each stage. HungerDeal is an android application that eases out the process of selecting the best food delivery application by comparing the best prices and delivery time of the food items, and this is done by real-time data scraping.

Better Genics | A Speculative Design

A portal that helps you plan your child's health before its birth. It collects parent's DNA samples and then finds any traces of hereditary diseases in your potential child. Also, the AI-based model takes into the geographical and socio-economic factors into account to see if some other changes are maybe necessary. This project was a part of the course Human Centered AI and involved user research and re-iterative designing for improving AI interactivity.

HMIS | Usability Testing

HMIS refers to the Health Information Management Information System. HMIS is a web-based interface to facilitate the process of data collection, data management, and data reporting and analysis from the district to the state level and then the national level. This project involved extensive user research at the PHC level and then suggesting design changes at the portal. The changes were also presented and appreciated by the National HMIS officer.
